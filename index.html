Use + Storage

Simple daily facial skincare directions.

Apply 1–2 pumps to clean skin once daily for the first week. If tolerated well after one week, use once or twice daily. Follow with moisturizer.

If used during the day, apply sunscreen after your skincare routine.

Keep refrigerated when not in use. For external facial use only. Patch test before use. Avoid direct contact with eyes. Discontinue use if irritation occurs.
